Plant Care Tips

Why Leaves Turn Yellow

Yellowing is usually about light or water balance — not doom. Start by checking soil moisture and light exposure.

  • Check drainage
  • Adjust light first
  • Water deeply, less often

Watering Isn’t the Problem — Light Is

Most indoor plants struggle from insufficient light. Move closer to a window before changing your watering routine.

  • Observe leaf size
  • Rotate weekly
  • Avoid dark corners
